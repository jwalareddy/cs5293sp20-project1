Narendra Modi, born 17 September, 1950 is an Indian politician serving as the 14th and current Prime Minister of India since 2014. Narendra Modi was the Chief Minister of Gujarat from 2001 to 2014 and is the Member of Parliament for Varanasi. Modi is a member of the Bharatiya Janata Party (BJP) and of the Rashtriya Swayamsevak Sangh (RSS), a Hindu nationalist volunteer organisation. Narendra Modi is the first prime minister outside of the Indian National Congress to win two consecutive terms with a full majority and the second to complete five years in office.
